The concept

Supply Line is a turn-based, two to four players territorial strategy game.


Basic rules

At each turn, opponents can use their new supply of troups to conquer or reinforce their positions. Troups and territories are lost if they are cut from the supplies provided by a nearby city more than one turn. A territory is harder to conquer if defended and easier if surrounded or unsupplied.
